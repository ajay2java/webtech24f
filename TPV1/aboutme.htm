About the Website Creator

Hi! My name is Ajay Subramanian, and I am a current senior at Babson College, concentrating in Business Analytics & Technology Entrepreneruship. I am currently in the process of creating this website during my Web Technologies class this semester. My motivation for creating this website is to showcase my artistic imaginations as a rising animator. I first got into animation back in 2022 when I created simple 2-D animations through my simple video editor. Fastforwarding to summer 2024, I grew my skills into become proficient with Adobe Illustrator & Adobe Animation to start creating more advanced 2-D animations. While I'm clearly not very advanced at this moment, I still find joy in showcasing how far I've come, especially in learning a skill I was once afraid of. For me personally, I want this website to send a message that you can do anything by which you put your mind to! Nothing is impossible, whether it be coding or creating video animations!
