Needed Softwares:
Software Name Reason
Adobe Illustrator To draw out the character, part-by-part. Adobe Illustrator: ($22.99 / month) + Adobe Character Animatior ($22.99 / month)
Adobe Character Animator To 'rig' the character, or give it the ability to move before recording clips of it.
Video Editor To add sound effects to the exported video from Animate.
Sum: [$45.98 / month] + Video Editor (should be free)

A Note about Different Softwares:

While I do understand that Adobe's softwares may be pricy, for any other softwares you choose to use, the same foundational concepts shown on my website still apply to those. Although, you may need to consult additional tutorials to get familiar with the exact intricacies of the software you choose.
