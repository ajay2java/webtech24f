1) Using Transparent Pictures thinking you can animate them



Slide 1 of 3

You cannot just expect to be able to animate without putting in effort in the part of drawing the character part-by-part. I know this may sound inimidating, but trust me, it's actually enjoyable if you try and not worry so much if you were to fail. Failure happens to all of us. My first animations were not very good, but as I continue to draw, animate, and DEBUG my mistakes, I only become better & my confidence grew! You cannot use transparent images as the limbs are not separate, making the Character Animate software unable to separate the parts of the character, instead viewing the image as a whole.
